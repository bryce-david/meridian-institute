1. Who We Are

Meridian Institute of AI ("Meridian," "we," "us," or "our") operates the website located at meridianinstituteai.com. We provide AI education courses and certification programs.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information about you when you use our website and services. By creating an account or using our site, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. What Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

  • Account registration: Your full name, email address, and password when you create an account.
  • Course activity: Which modules you have completed, assessment scores, and certificates earned.
  • Communications: If you contact us by email, we retain those communications.
  • Notification sign-ups: If you submit your email to be notified about upcoming courses, we store that email address.

Information collected automatically

  • Usage data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, and navigation paths through the course material.
  • Device information: Browser type, operating system, and screen resolution.
  • Local storage: We use your browser's local storage to save your course progress between sessions on the same device.
  • IP address: Collected automatically by our hosting provider (Netlify) for security and analytics purposes.

Information we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect payment card information directly. All payment processing (when applicable) is handled by Stripe, a PCI-compliant payment processor.
  • We do not collect government identification numbers, social security numbers, or financial account details.
  • We do not use facial recognition or biometric data.
  • We do not track your activity on other websites.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide the service: Creating and managing your account, tracking course progress, issuing certificates, and delivering course content.
  • To communicate with you: Sending account-related emails such as password resets, course completion confirmations, and important service updates. We do not send marketing emails without your explicit consent.
  • To improve the platform: Understanding which course sections are most used or where users stop, to improve content quality and structure.
  • To maintain security: Detecting and preventing fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access to accounts.
  • To issue verifiable certificates: Associating your name and completion status with a unique certificate ID that can be publicly verified.
  • To comply with legal obligations: Responding to lawful requests from government authorities when legally required.

We will never use your information to serve you advertisements, sell your data to data brokers, or build advertising profiles about you.

4. How We Store and Protect Your Data

Your account data is stored using Google Firebase, a cloud platform operated by Google LLC. Firebase provides industry-standard security including:

  • Encryption of data in transit using TLS/HTTPS.
  • Encryption of data at rest.
  • Access controlled by security rules we configure to ensure users can only access their own data.
  • Authentication handled by Firebase Authentication, which stores passwords as secure cryptographic hashes — we never see or store your plain-text password.

Our website is hosted on Netlify, which provides automatic HTTPS encryption for all pages. Our domain is secured with an SSL certificate.

While we implement strong security measures, no system is completely immune to security risks. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your account.

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party.

We share information only in the following limited circumstances:

Service providers

We use the following third-party services that may process your data on our behalf:

  • Google Firebase (Google LLC) — account authentication and database storage. Firebase Privacy Policy.
  • Netlify — website hosting. Netlify Privacy Policy.
  • Stripe (when payment processing is enabled) — payment processing. Stripe does not share your payment data with us beyond transaction confirmation. Stripe Privacy Policy.

All service providers are contractually required to use your data only to provide services to us and not for their own independent purposes.

Legal requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, court order, or government authority, or if we believe disclosure is necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of Meridian, our users, or the public.

Business transfers

If Meridian is acquired, merged, or undergoes a significant change in ownership, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you before your information is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

6. Cookies and Local Storage

We use browser local storage (not traditional cookies) to save your course progress on your device. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to our servers unless you are logged in, in which case it is synced to your account in Firebase.

Firebase Authentication uses a small number of necessary cookies to maintain your logged-in session. These are functional cookies required to operate the service — they are not advertising or tracking cookies.

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other third-party advertising or behavioral tracking technologies.

7. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Meridian Institute of AI is designed to serve users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

If you are under 13, you may not create an account or submit personal information to this site. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has created an account, please contact us immediately at privacy@meridianinstituteai.com and we will promptly delete the account and all associated data.

For users between the ages of 13 and 17, we encourage parental awareness of their use of this site. We do not market to minors, collect sensitive personal information from them beyond what is necessary for account creation, or use their data for advertising purposes.

8. Your Rights

Regardless of where you live, you have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: You may request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: You may request that we delete your account and all associated personal data. Note that certificates that have already been issued and verified publicly may retain a non-personal verification record.
  • Portability: You may request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Objection: You may object to certain uses of your data.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@meridianinstituteai.com. We will respond within 30 days. We may require you to verify your identity before processing your request.

9. European Users (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) or equivalent laws.

Legal basis for processing: We process your personal data on the following legal bases:

  • Contract: Processing necessary to provide the service you signed up for, including account management and course delivery.
  • Legitimate interests: Improving our platform, maintaining security, and preventing fraud.
  • Consent: For any marketing communications, which you may withdraw at any time.
  • Legal obligation: Compliance with applicable laws.

You have the right to lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority if you believe we have not handled your data in accordance with the law.

International transfers: Your data is stored on Google Firebase servers, which may be located in the United States. Google LLC participates in the EU-U.S. Data Privacy Framework, providing appropriate safeguards for international transfers.

10. California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA):

  • The right to know what personal information we collect, use, disclose, or sell.
  • The right to delete personal information we have collected from you.
  • The right to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.
  • The right to non-discrimination for exercising your CCPA rights.
  • The right to correct inaccurate personal information.
  • The right to limit use of sensitive personal information (we do not collect sensitive personal information as defined by the CPRA).

To exercise your California privacy rights, contact us at privacy@meridianinstituteai.com.

11. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, we will delete your account and personal information within 30 days, with the following exceptions:

  • We may retain anonymized, non-identifiable usage statistics that cannot be linked back to you.
  • We may retain information required to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce our agreements.
  • Certificate verification records may be retained in a minimized form (certificate ID and completion date only, without personal identifiers) for the integrity of the verification system.
