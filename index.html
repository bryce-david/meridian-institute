FAQ

Common questions.

Are the courses actually free?

Yes. All beginner and foundational content is completely free — no credit card, no trial period. The advanced domain tracks (Finance, Law, Healthcare, etc.) are also free to start. Meridian's premise is that world-class AI education shouldn't cost thousands of dollars.

Do employers recognize Meridian certificates?

Meridian certificates are verifiable — each one has a unique ID that links to a live certificate page employers can check. They demonstrate real knowledge, not just course completion. That said, certificates are a floor: pair yours with actual projects and you'll stand out far more than a credential alone.

How long does each course take?

Each course has 10 modules. At roughly 15–20 minutes per module, most people finish in 3–5 hours spread over a few days. There are no deadlines — learn at whatever pace works for you.

Do I need a technical background?

No. The beginner track assumes zero prior knowledge — it's designed for anyone curious about AI. Even the advanced domain tracks (Law, Finance, Healthcare) focus on applying AI within those fields, not on writing code. The machine learning and AI agents tracks go deeper technically, but still build from first principles.

Where should I start if I'm completely new to AI?

Start with the Foundations of AI course — it's free, has no prerequisites, and covers everything you need to understand before going into any domain. From there, pick the track that matches your career or field.

Can AI courses actually help me get a job?

AI skills are now a differentiator in almost every professional field. Lawyers, analysts, doctors, and marketers who understand AI are more hireable and more productive than those who don't. Meridian's courses are built around exactly those real-world applications — not theoretical exercises. The certificates give you something concrete to show; the knowledge is what actually lands the job.

How many courses are there?

20 full courses, each with 10 modules and a final quiz for certificate eligibility — over 200 modules in total. Tracks cover AI fundamentals, tool mastery (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity), and domain applications across Finance, Law, Healthcare, Cybersecurity, Governance, Education, Politics, Society, Strategy, Safety, Machine Learning, Agents, Prompt Engineering, Research, APIs, and more.
